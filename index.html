How This System Works

1. Upload Document

The user uploads an offer letter received via email or WhatsApp.

2. AI Analysis

AI extracts text, detects keywords, salary promises, company format, and suspicious patterns.

3. Company Check

Company name and details are cross-verified using trusted data sources.

4. Final Result

The system generates a verification result with risk indication.
